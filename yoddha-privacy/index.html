Privacy Policy for Yoddha Race Timer

Last updated: December 28, 2024

1. Information Collection

The Yoddha Race Timer app is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

2. Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Race completion times
  • Zone performance metrics
  • Training records
  • Workout statistics

3. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security is maintained by your device's built-in security features. We recommend keeping your device's operating system and security features up to date.

4. App Permissions

Our app requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage access (to save your workout data locally)
  • No internet access required
  • No location access required
  • No camera or media access required

5. Updates

Updates to the app will be distributed through the Google Play Store. This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect any changes in our practices.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.
